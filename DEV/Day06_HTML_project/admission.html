Admission Procedure in The Asian School

Are you considering enrolling your child in The Asian School? Here is an overview of the admission procedure to help you understand the process.



Enquiry Form

The admission procedure at The Asian School starts with the Enquiry form. This is where you express your interest in admitting your child to the school. You can fill out the Enquiry form online or visit the school in person to submit the form.



Guidance from Counsellors

Once you have submitted the Enquiry form, our team of counsellors will guide you regarding all the inquiries you may have about the school, curriculum, facilities, and more. They will provide you with all the necessary information to help you make an informed decision about your child’s education.



Registration

After you have gathered all the information you need and are ready to proceed with the admission process, you will need to complete the registration formalities. This usually involves submitting the required documents along with the registration fee.



Entrance Test

Once the registration process is complete, the next step is for the student to appear for an entrance test. The purpose of the entrance test is to assess the student’s knowledge and skills in various subjects. This test helps the school determine the student’s academic level and place them in the appropriate grade.
